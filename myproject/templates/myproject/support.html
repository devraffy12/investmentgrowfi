Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make a deposit?

You can make a deposit by going to the Dashboard and clicking on the "Deposit" button. Choose your preferred payment method and follow the instructions.

How long does it take to process withdrawals?

Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours during business days. Processing times may vary depending on your chosen payment method.

How do daily payouts work?

Daily payouts are automatically credited to your account balance every 24 hours based on your active investments. You can view your payout history in the Records section.

How do I refer friends and earn commissions?

Share your referral code found in your Profile page. When someone registers using your code and makes their first investment, you'll earn a referral commission.
